Felicia draws inspiration from design languages found in cultures worldwide. Experienced in graphic design, illustration, animation, and photography, she works in Adobe Illustrator, After Effects, Photoshop, and Premiere Pro.

She is pursuing a Bachelors Degree in Digital Communication Arts at Oregon State University and is a student employee on the OSU Ecampus Animation & Design Team.

Felicia focuses on creating designs that connect humans to information, translating brand identity clearly, enhancing user experience with clean layouts, and capturing what the eye naturally seeks.
